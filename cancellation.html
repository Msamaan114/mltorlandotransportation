Cancellation Policy

We understand that travel plans can change. Our cancellation policy is designed to be flexible and fair to our guests while protecting our drivers’ time and schedules.

Flexible Cancellations

  • Cancel up to 24 hours before your trip for a full refund.
  • No fees for changes made 24+ hours in advance.
  • No fees for changing flights due to flight delays or cancellation.

Late Cancellations

  • Cancellations within 2–24 hours of pickup incur a 50% charge.
  • This helps cover the driver’s time and reserved schedule.

No-Shows & Less Than 2 Hours

  • No refund for cancellations less than 2 hours before pickup.
  • Full charge applies for no-shows at pickup.

If your plans change, please contact us as soon as possible by phone, WhatsApp or email so we can adjust your reservation and help you avoid unnecessary fees.
