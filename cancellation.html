Cancellation Policy

We understand that travel plans can change. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair to you and to our drivers.

Flexible Cancellations

  • Cancel up to 24 hours before your trip for a full refund.
  • No fees for changes made 24+ hours in advance.
  • No fees for changing flights due to flight delays or cancellation.

Late Cancellations

  • Cancellations within 2–24 hours incur a 50% charge.
  • This helps cover the driver’s time and schedule.

No-Shows & Less Than 2 Hours

  • No refund for cancellations less than 2 hours before pickup.
  • Full charge for no-shows at pickup.
