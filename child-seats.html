Complimentary Child Car Seats

Traveling with little ones? We can provide rear-facing infant seats, forward-facing car seats and booster seats at no extra cost when requested in advance, so your family can ride safely and comfortably.

  • Rear-facing infant seats for babies and smaller toddlers (on request).
  • Forward-facing car seats for toddlers and young children.
  • Booster seats for older children who still need a height boost for the seat belt.
  • Seats are provided free of charge, but must be requested during booking so we can prepare the correct number and type of seats for your trip.

For safety, please share each child’s age and approximate weight in the booking form notes, so we can match the right seat type to each passenger.
