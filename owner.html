Meet the Owner

MLT Orlando Transportation is locally owned and operated. You’ll often be greeted by the owner himself, ensuring a professional and personal experience from the very first ride.

With years of experience serving Orlando visitors and business travelers, the owner of MLT Orlando Transportation understands what guests need after a long flight: a friendly face, a clean vehicle, and a smooth ride to their destination.

From airport transfers to theme park trips and cruise departures, we treat each reservation with care. Flight delays and changes happen — we monitor your arrival and work with you to keep your pickup simple and stress-free.

Whether you’re a family visiting the parks or a frequent business traveler, you can expect on-time service, clear communication and a professional appearance every time.
