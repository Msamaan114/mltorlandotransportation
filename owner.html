Meet the Owner

MLT Orlando Transportation is locally owned and operated. You’ll often be greeted by the owner himself, ensuring a professional and personal experience from the very first ride.

As a local Orlando driver, the owner of MLT Orlando Transportation has spent years helping families, business travelers and cruise guests get where they need to go comfortably and on time.

After a long flight, guests appreciate seeing a familiar, professional face waiting in the arrivals area. From there, you can relax while we handle the bags, navigation and traffic — straight to your hotel, vacation home, cruise port or meeting.

We closely monitor flight status and stay in touch by phone or WhatsApp, so if delays or changes happen, we adjust your pickup whenever possible. Communication and reliability are at the heart of every trip.

Whether you’re here for a family vacation at Disney and Universal, or in town for important meetings, our goal is simple: provide a smooth, safe, and stress-free ride every time.
