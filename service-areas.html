Service Areas

MLT Orlando Transportation mainly serves the greater Orlando area, with most trips to and from the major airports, theme parks and cruise ports. If you’re not sure whether we can serve your location, just contact us and we’ll be happy to confirm.

Orlando & Surrounding Areas

Service across Orlando and nearby communities, including popular hotel and resort zones around International Drive, Lake Buena Vista and Kissimmee. We also cover many vacation home neighborhoods in the greater Orlando area.

Airports: MCO & SFB

Frequent service from Orlando International Airport (MCO) and Orlando Sanford International Airport (SFB) to local hotels, vacation rentals and attractions. Meet-and-greet pickups available at arrivals.

Theme Parks & Attractions

Direct service to Walt Disney World, Universal Orlando Resort, SeaWorld, and other major attractions across the Orlando area. Perfect for families with strollers, luggage and child car seat needs.

Cape Canaveral & Coast

Trips to Cape Canaveral and nearby cruise terminals, ideal for guests traveling between Orlando, MCO and Port Canaveral. We can coordinate pickup times with your cruise schedule for both departure and return.

If you need transportation outside these areas, send us your pickup and drop-off locations on the booking form and we’ll let you know if we can accommodate your request.
