Why Choose MLT Orlando Transportation

There are many ways to get around Orlando. Our guests choose MLT Orlando Transportation because we combine local experience, clear communication and family-friendly service in every ride.

  • Local Orlando driver who knows the airports, resorts and cruise routes.
  • Private, pre-booked rides so you skip taxi lines and rideshare surge pricing.
  • Clear communication by phone, email or WhatsApp before pickup.
  • Flat-rate quotes for common routes like MCO ↔ Disney and MCO ↔ Cape Canaveral.
  • Friendly help with luggage so families can focus on enjoying their trip.
  • Complimentary child car seats (rear-facing, forward-facing and boosters) when requested in advance.
  • Flexible cancellations with fair policies that respect your time and our drivers.

Perfect for Families

From the moment you land, we’re ready to help with strollers, luggage and child seats. Pre-booked rides mean less stress for parents and more time for the fun parts of your trip.

Reliable for Business

On-time pickups, professional appearance and direct routes to hotels, meetings and private airports make us a dependable choice for business travelers.

Easy for Cruise Guests

We coordinate pickup times with your cruise schedule, offering smooth transfers between Orlando, MCO and Port Canaveral so your vacation starts and ends comfortably.

From family moments to business success, we’re here to get you there safely, comfortably and on time.
